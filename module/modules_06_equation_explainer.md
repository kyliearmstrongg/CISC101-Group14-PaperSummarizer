# Module 06: Equation Explainer

## Logic & Steps
1. **Equation Identification**
    - Scan all sections for mathematical equations, models, or formulae.

2. **Plain Language Explanation**
    - For each important equation, succinctly explain its purpose in the context of the study.
    - *No external interpretation; explanations must be based strictly on provided text.*

3. **Technical Fidelity**
    - Preserve mathematical symbols and terminology.
    - Maintain factuality and source traceability.

## Output
- List of identified equations with short plain-English explanations, all directly supported by paper content.
